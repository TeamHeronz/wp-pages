Introducing Team Heronz

A special project requires special people behind it. Meet the people who make Heronz come true.

Tutoring

Our tutors are the face of Heronz. Working towards passing knowledge across, our tutors share the belief that learning is for everyone. All tutors at Heronz are multilingual to dissolve the language barrier.

Curriculum

Our curriculum coordinators guarantee Heronz with quality content. The Division, under the management of Yoon Ko, is currently working on the Heronz Mathematics Curriculum on LyX.

Web Development

Our developers make Heronz happen, anywhere and anytime, for free. From Web Design, responsible for colour, typography, layout, and images, to Web Programming, using HTML5, PHP, CSS, and JavaScript, to Customer Support, the online platform requires a multitude of disciplines to converge. This division is managed by Lucia Kim.

Team Heronz needs your help

Heronz is always lacking contributers. Like to teach? Want to test your knowledge? Interested in design? Know how to write HTML5? Heronz has a place for you.
