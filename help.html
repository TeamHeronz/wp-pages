Welcome to our Help Centre

Frequently asked questions

What does Heronz do?

Heronz offers personalised one-to-one online tutoring. We currently have programmes in Mathematics and English.

Who runs Heronz?

Heronz is made and maintained by Team Heronz. All members are students studying in New Zealand.

Is Heronz truly free?

Yes! Heronz believes in education for everyone, and that learning should be barrier-free, including cost.

How do begin learning?

Enrol at Heronz through the form. You will soon be connected with a tutor. Enter the Heronz Classroom using the tutor code.

Contact us directly

Do you are having technical issues? Want to talk to us before joining Heronz? Is there something else you want to tell us? Whatever you have to say, reach us through the contact form below. We will be in touch as soon as possible.
