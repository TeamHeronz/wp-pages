Tailored learning for everyone.

Because every curriculum is different in different schools, and everyone learns at different paces, all classes are personalised at Heronz. Start your search here.

Mathematics

Our Mathematics Programmes are based on the Heronz Mathematics Curriculum. The Curriculum takes a step-by-step approach to topics in Numeracy, Geometry, Algebra, Statistics, Functions, and more.

General

This programme is for students wanting general improvements in their mathematics, following the New Zealand curriculum. A range of topics are covered, including NCEA, and also help with homework and competition preparations.

International

This programme is for students who want to learn an overseas mathematics curriculum, or would like to reference respective topics from their background while studying the New Zealand currciculum. Mathematics tutors at Heronz have a background in Chinese, Korean, and Vietnamese curriculums.

IB Mathematics

This programme is specifically for students taking IB Mathematics, Standard or Higher Level. Tutors at Heronz will help you understand the concepts, as well as tips and tricks to solve the problem faster.

English

Our English Programmes are for both local students learning English as a first language, and international students learning English as a second language. English is taught in four aspects of reading, writing, listening, and speaking interweaved together. Let us know if you want to be tutored in your native language.

Comprehensive

This programme covers all four reading, writing, listening, speaking, as well as English grammar.

Grammar

This programme focuses on English grammar, with particular attention to reading and writing.

Conversation

This programme focuses on conversing in English, using listening and speaking skills.
